About Us

Sacred Heart Convent High School and Cluny Convent High School, two notable institutions for website information. Sacred Heart is a co-ed, English-medium secondary school following the CBSE curriculum, established by the Fatima Sisters in 1996 and preparing students for the SSC examination. Cluny Convent High School is another prominent option, with some information available from its official website.



School Information

The course of studies of school prepares students for CBSE Examinations. Central Board of Secondary Education All India . English is the medium of instruction .Marathi and Hindi are taught as second and third languages .Reports on conduct, application and progress are issued after each of the unit tests and the examinations . Parents are expected to sign these reports and return them to the School.


Board of Management

The Board of Management is committed to provide quality education to promote intellectual ,social and cultural vivacity among its learners .The board advocates Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation with an emphasis on holistic development of learners.


Brief of School

Fatima Convent School, Udyamnagar, Ratnagiri came into being in 2019 and is conducted by the Fatima Sisters . The school started with the Pre-Primary section and 1st to 5th standard subsequently, every year a new standard will be added. The school aims at training students to take up their place in society as responsible citizens and to build a new and better world. This is a co-education school and open to pupils of all classes and creed. Its aim is to impart sound education, instill praiseworthy character ,create balanced judgement and robust physique so as to enable its pupils to develop into useful citizens . It is owned by the Society of Sisters of Our Lady of Fatima which belongs to the Christian Religious Minority group and therefore enjoys the rights and privileges guaranteed by Article 30(i) of the Constitution of India .The Society of the Sisters of Our Lady of Fatima, conducts several such schools in India.
